
# A Practical, People‑First Platform
**Focus:** Accountability • Affordability • Opportunity • Community

## A. Accountability & Transparency
- Plain‑language budgets & calendars for public review
- Independent oversight of key programs
- Open data (non‑sensitive) to reduce public‑records wait times

## B. Lower Everyday Costs
- Identify and cut avoidable/nuisance fees
- Streamline processes that add time and cost
- Use competitive bidding and scale purchasing where appropriate

## C. Lower Housing Costs
- Predictable, timely approvals for safe, code‑compliant building
- Encourage infill/adaptive reuse where infrastructure exists
- Protect homeowners and renters with clear disclosures and anti‑fraud enforcement

## D. Better Jobs
- Employer‑aligned training, apprenticeships, short‑cycle credentials
- Practical help for small businesses (simple checklists, fast‑track for routine filings)
- Local supplier development and fair competition

## E. Strong Borders (Process Focus)
- Secure, orderly, and lawful border management
- Targeted enforcement based on risk and repeat offenses
- Efficient case handling to reduce backlogs and uncertainty

## F. Protect Children & Pursue Justice
- Zero tolerance for child exploitation
- Lawful transparency in investigations and due process
- Victim‑centered services and prevention education
